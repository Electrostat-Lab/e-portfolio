= Clinical E-portfolio

[.subtitle]
Built by link:https://github.com/pavly-gerges[Dr. Pavly G.]

== Preface
An electronic portfolio built from real case scenarios; allocated during the clinical rotations in the Emergency Department, Critical Care units, and Surgical ORs. It houses presentation of the cases and their extensive analysis based on the pure science behind the pathophysiology, the biophysics involved, and the timeline of the cases. 

At the end of each case, a discussion section is included to wrap-up the case findings, the scientific basis and present an overall reflection on them, and how exactly they could be utilized in the clinical rotations to enhance the medical practice. 

== Content
The content is typically a constellation of case studies, physical theories of biophysics, and clinical maps. All of the formerly stated materials support the medical and surgical science, and practice.
